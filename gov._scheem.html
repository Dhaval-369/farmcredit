Government Schemes for Farmers in India

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Objective: Provides direct income support to small and marginal farmers.

Benefits: ₹6,000 annually in three equal installments directly into bank accounts.

Eligibility: All landholding farmer families, subject to exclusion criteria.

More Info

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Objective: Offers affordable crop insurance against natural calamities.

Benefits: Covers risks from pre-sowing to post-harvest, providing claim amounts for crop loss.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas.

More Info

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY)

Objective: Provides social security to small and marginal farmers.

Benefits: Minimum fixed pension of ₹3,000 per month after age 60.

Eligibility: Farmers aged 18-40 with cultivable land up to 2 hectares.

More Info

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Objective: Enhances irrigation coverage and water use efficiency.

Benefits: End-to-end solutions in irrigation supply chains.

Eligibility: All farmers, with a focus on rain-fed areas.

More Info

Soil Health Card Scheme

Objective: Promotes soil testing for nutrient status.

Benefits: Provides soil health cards with fertilizer recommendations.

Eligibility: All farmers.

More Info

e-National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

Objective: Creates a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

Benefits: Transparent online trading and better price discovery.

Eligibility: All farmers and traders.

More Info

Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centres (ACABC)

Objective: Provides extension services through agri-entrepreneurs.

Benefits: Training and credit support for agri-clinics and agri-business centers.

Eligibility: Graduates in agriculture and allied subjects.

More Info

National Livestock Mission (NLM)

Objective: Improves livestock production systems.

Benefits: Focuses on quality feed and fodder for sustainable growth.

Eligibility: All livestock farmers.

More Info

Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)

Objective: Promotes dairy farming through financial assistance.

Benefits: Supports modern dairy farms and milk procurement infrastructure.

Eligibility: Individuals, groups, and organizations in dairy farming.

More Info

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM)

Objective: Provides financial and water security through solar energy.

Benefits: Supports solar pumps and grid-connected solar power plants.

Eligibility: All farmers.
